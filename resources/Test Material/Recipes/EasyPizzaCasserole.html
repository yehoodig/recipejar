Easy Pizza Casserole

Notes:

From betterrecipes.com.

You will need:
  • 1 1/2 lb hamburger
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped green pepper
  • 2 minced cloves
  • 16 oz can spaghetti sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese
  • For the Batter:
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Parmesan Cheese

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Brown hamburger meat, drain. Stir in onion, green pepper, garlic and spaghetti sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes. In mixing bowl combine milk, oil and eggs. Add flour and salt.
Pour meat mixture into 13x9, cover with mozzarella, top with batter. Sprinkle with parmesan. Bake for 25-30 min until deep golden brown.
