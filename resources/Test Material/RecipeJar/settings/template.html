Notes:

A little about this recipe,
where I got it why I like it, etc.
Makes 1 serving.

You will need:

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • 1 Tbsp Jam
  • 1 Tbsp Peanut Butter

Procedure:

Evenly spread the Jam on one of the bread slices, and the Peanut Butter on the other.
Apply the condiment coated side of one of the bread slices to the condiment coated side of the other.
If desired, cut the sandwich into geometric shapes before enjoying.
