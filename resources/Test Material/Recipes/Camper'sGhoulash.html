Camper's Ghoulash

Notes:

Old family recipe. Maybe not world class,
but satisfying none the less.

You will need:

  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 bag Egg noodles
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 lb 1lb ground beef

Chop the Onion as Saute with a little oil. Add the beef and cook over medium-high heat until browned.
Drain, the oil. Add the egg noodles and smear the mushroom soup over the top. Add two cans water.
Cover and simmer 20-30 minutes or until the noodles are cooked. Mix well, add salt, pepper and worcestershire sauce to taste.
