Beer Batter

Notes:

Found online by searching for beer batter recipe.
I think the site was fishex.com or something...

You will need:

  • 1 can Beer
  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Procedure:

Mix well.
Heat about 2 inches of oil to 375.
Coat whatever is to be battered in flour,
dip into the batter and then cook in the oil until done.
