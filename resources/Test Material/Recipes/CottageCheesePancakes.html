Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Notes:

Joy of Cooking pg 797

You will need:

  • 1 1/3 Cups Flour
  • 1/3 Cup Sugar
  • 2 tsps Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup milk
  • 1 Cup Cottage Cheese
  • 3 Tbsps melted butter
  • 2 Count egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 Egg whites

Procedure:

Mix all dry ingredients.
Mix all wet ingredients, except egg whites.
Mix wet into dry.
Beat egg whites, until stiff.
Fold into batter.
