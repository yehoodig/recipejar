Cranberry Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce

Notes:

From the recipe rack at the commissary.
You will need:
  • 1 cup fresh cranberries
  • 1/2 cup orange marmalade
  • 4 slices unfrosted cinnamon bread, toasted
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce (ice cream topping), warmed

Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a small saucepan combine cranberries, orange marmalade, and 2 Tbsp water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer uncovered 8-10 minutes or until cranberries pop, stirring occasionally.

Meanwhile, cut bread into 1-inch pieces. Place in ungreased 8x8 baking pan.

In medium mixing bowl stir together eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla. Stir in cranberry mixture. Pour over bread.

Bake 50-55 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool slightly. Serve warm, with warm caramel sauce.

Serves 6.
