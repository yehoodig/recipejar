Cheesy Chicken Breasts

Notes:

You will need:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Lemon Pepper
Cut a slit into each of the thawed chicken breasts.
Mix Lemon Pepper and Mayo to taste, and in sufficient quantity to coat the breasts inside and out.
Smear the mixture over and through each breast.
Stuff breasts with cheese.
Barbecue or broil until done. Melt extra cheese over the top.
