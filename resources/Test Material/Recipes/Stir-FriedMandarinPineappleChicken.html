Stir-Fried Mandarin Pineapple Chicken

Notes:

Off of the Vip stir-fry vegetables package.
You will need:
  • 1 14oz package stir-fry vegetables
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 12oz (2 cups) cubed chicken
  • 8oz can pineapple chunks
Heat oil in wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken; stir-fry 2-3 minutes. Add veggies, stir-fry3-4 more minutes. Fold in pineapple. Heat thoroughly.
