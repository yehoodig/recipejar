Black Bean Pasta

Notes:

From betterrecipes.com.

You will need:
  • 4 Ribs celery, chopped
  • 2 Cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can black beans (undrained)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (drained)
  • 8 ounces of penne or ziti pasta, cooked
  • Parmesan cheese

Heat oil in pan. Saute celery for 2 min, add garlic. Continue to cook for 3 min. Add beans and tomatoes. Add pasta. Cook for 3 more min. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Serve?
