Apple Sauce Cobbler

Notes:

Apple sauce doctored up to taste like apple cobbler.
Erin made me write it down. :)

You will need:

  • 2 Cups Apple Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Vanilla
  • 3 Tbsps Butter
  • 1/8 Cup Brown sugar
  • 1/8 Cup Milk
  • white sugar (to taste)
Heat and stir the brown sugar, butter, cinnamon and vanilla over medium high heat until combined.
Stir in Apple sauce, heat until warmed. Stir in milk. Serve warm.
