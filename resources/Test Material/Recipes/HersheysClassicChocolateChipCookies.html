Hershey's Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Notes:

Back of the Hershey's® chocolate chip package.

Makes ~5doz

You will need:

  • 2 1/4 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Cup butter (2 sticks)
  • 3/4 Cup white sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 Count eggs
  • 2 Cups chocolate chips (1 12oz package)
  • 1 Cup chopped nuts (optional)
Heat oven to 375°F.
Stir together flour, soda and salt, set aside.
Beat butter, sugars and vanilla together.
Add eggs; beat well.
Gradually add flour mixture, beat well.
Stir in chocolate chips (and nuts).

Bake 8-10min

For pan cookies: Grease 9x13, pour in batter. Bake 20min.
